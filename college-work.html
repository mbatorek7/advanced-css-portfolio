College Assignments

I attended Valparaiso University from 2017 to 2021. I started out with a major in Computer Engineering; however, I quickly realized I enjoyed not only hardware, but the software side of technology as well. After my first semester, I became a double major in Computer Science and Engineering. I studied a plethora of different topics, such as mobile computing, embedded microcontrollers, computer architecture, database management systems, and networks.
